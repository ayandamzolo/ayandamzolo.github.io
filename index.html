About Me

I'm Mitchelle A. Mzolo, a passionate Full-Stack Developer with a knack for building robust back-end systems and creating intuitive, seamless user experiences. My journey began at Tshwane University of Technology (TUT), where I specialized in C++ and developed a solid foundation in software development. Since then, I’ve broadened my expertise, gaining hands-on experience with industry-leading tools and frameworks. Currently, I specialize in full-stack development, working with technologies like Laravel, Inertia.js, Vue.js, JavaScript, and PHP. My projects reflect a blend of technical precision and a user-centric approach, showcasing my ability to build scalable, efficient, and user-friendly applications. Beyond technical skills, I bring a collaborative mindset and a problem-solving approach to every project. I love working with teams to solve complex challenges and am dedicated to continuous learning to keep up with the latest industry trends. My portfolio includes a mix of front-end and back-end projects, demonstrating my versatility and commitment to delivering high-quality solutions.
